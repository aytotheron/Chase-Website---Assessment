Here are some Frequently Asked Questions from The Chase!

How are the Chasers chosen for each show?

During the show, contestants do not know which Chaser they face until they walk out; even the host, Bradley Walsh, is kept in the dark.

Do Chasers get bonuses if they catch constestants?

No, Chasers do not earn bonuses if they catch a contestant/contestants as Chasers already have a paid salary and don't recieve bonuses.

What happens if the Chaser catches all contestants?

If the Chaser catches all contestants, the contestants are reunited back together as a team and nominate one person to return for the Final Chase for a grand prize for £1,000 for each contestant.

How does the Head-to-Head round work?

Contestants must answer questions correctly in order not to be caught by the Chaser. The contestant has a choice of playing for their earned amount from the Cash Builder, a higher offer to the Chaser, or a lower offer which is closer to home.

How do Chasers remember everything?

Many Chasers are professional quizzers who constantly study and compete in international championships to keep their knowledge nice and sharp.
